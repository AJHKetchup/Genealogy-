# Staged Claim Draft

## Proposed Claim

The 1889 civil birth entry 513 identifies Daniel Pulgar Arriagada as a male child born on 22 June 1889 at Calle de Colon and names Jose del Carmen Pulgar and Juana de Dios Arriagada de Pulgar as his parents.

## Literal Source Support

```text
Nombre: Pulgar Arriagada Daniel
Fecha: Junio veinte i dos de mil ochocientos ochenta i nueve, a las cuatro i media de la tarde
Lugar: Calle de Colon
Nombre del padre: Jose del Carmen Pulgar
Nombre de la madre: Juana de Dios Arriagada de Pulgar
Nombre, etc., del compareciente:
Jose del Carmen Pulgar
Padre
```

## Translation

```text
Name: Daniel Pulgar Arriagada.
Birth: June 22, 1889, at 4:30 in the afternoon, Calle de Colon.
Father: Jose del Carmen Pulgar.
Mother: Juana de Dios Arriagada de Pulgar.
Informant: Jose del Carmen Pulgar, father.
```

## Interpretation

This is strong draft evidence for both a birth event and a parent-child relationship because the entry is complete and the father personally presented the birth.
