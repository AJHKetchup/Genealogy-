## Review Judgment

The evidence is highly relevant to a mother claim for the child recorded in entry 513, but it does not support this staged claim as written. The largest problem is identity: the visible child-name field does not appear to name `Isidoro del Carmen Diaz`. The second problem is the mother endpoint: the visible mother line is compatible with `Juana de Dios ... de Pulgar`, but `Amador` is not sufficiently secure for canonical use from this conversion and image review.

This should remain a proof hold rather than a promotion. The source likely contains useful direct evidence for the mother of entry 513's child, but the child identity and full mother name both need targeted conversion QA before any canonical claim is made.

Keep this claim on hold. Run targeted conversion QA for entry 513's child-name field and the full mother-name line, then revise or replace the staged mother claim only to the extent the visible source supports the corrected endpoints.
