## Literal Source Reading

The staged draft quotes the legal notice as naming:

```text
de aparente dominio de Darío
Pulgar Arriagada
```

The source packet gives the fuller literal context:

```text
Por resolución 283, 5 diciembre 2000, Serviu Región del Bío Bío ordenó
expropiación parcial inmueble ubicado en Línea Ferrea s/n°, Rol de Avalúo
N°2720-002, comuna Chiguayante, superficie 2.523,20 m2. de terreno, dentro polígono
y linderos señalados plano expropiación 2720-002, de aparente dominio de Darío
Pulgar Arriagada
```

Literal reading: a person named `Darío Pulgar Arriagada` is named as the apparent domain holder of a partially expropriated Chiguayante property in Resolution 283, promulgated 2000-12-05 and published 2001-01-15.

Interpretation kept separate: this supports a named-person identity candidate and an apparent-property-domain/legal-notice event. It does not prove title ownership, residence, kinship, or same-person identity with other Pulgar candidates.
