- The 1928 row is a staged/conversion source that requires proof review of dense row-column alignment.

## Ranked Conclusion

1. Most plausible same-person bridge: El Aguila `Dr. Dario Pulgar A.` and 1928 Highland Loch `Dario Pulgar A.`, age 39, `Medical Surgeon`.
2. Plausible but not directly bridged: El Aguila `Dr. Dario Pulgar A.` and 1918 `Darío J. Pulgar Arriagada`, if `A.` is confirmed as Arriagada or another source connects the doctor/fundo owner to the title-conferral person.
3. Possible name expansion, not proven: `Dario Jose Pulgar-Arriagada`, contingent on proving that `Darío J.` expands to Jose and that Pulgar Arriagada is the same doctor/fundo owner.
4. Low-confidence contextual match only: later canonical/staged `Darío Pulgar Arriagada` legal-notice person.
5. Unlikely same person: `Dario Arturo Pulgar` or `Dario Arturo Pulgar-Smith`, because the 1942-born `DARIO A PULGAR` / CV cluster is chronologically incompatible with the 1917 inheritance and 1928 medical-surgeon evidence.
6. Not supported: any Jose/Juana parent candidate as parent of Dr. Dario Pulgar A.
