# Source Packet: Dr Dario Pulgar Official Distributor Note

This packet stages the person-first evidence that `Dr Dario Pulgar` was named in an `El Aguila` company note as the company's official distributor for back issues and later subscription inquiries.

## Literal Support

```text
NOTAS DE LA EMPRESA.
LA EMPRESA A DESIGNADO A DR DARIO PULGA R A NUESTRO DISTRIBUIDOR
OFICIAL PAPA NUMEROS ATRASADOS SIRVAN DIRIGIRSE A NUESTRA DIRECCION EN
CONCEPCION . LUEGO SE HABRIRAN LAS SUSCRIPCIONES PARA LO CUAL
CONSULTEN CON NUESTRO DISTRIBUIDOR.
```

## Extracted Family Evidence

- The useful family evidence is the named role: `Dr Dario Pulgar` was designated as the company's official distributor.
- The note uses the honorific/title `DR` before Dario Pulgar's name.
- The note points readers seeking back issues to the company's address in Concepcion and says subscription inquiries should consult the distributor, but it does not state Dario Pulgar's own residence.
