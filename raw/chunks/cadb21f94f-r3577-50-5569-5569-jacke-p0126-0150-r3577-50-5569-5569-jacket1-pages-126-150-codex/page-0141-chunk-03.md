[Handwritten note in black ink]
W Colban.

Dr Rajchman to his great regret had no time to
deal with this question before his departure. He asked
me to do so on his behalf.

Are not all the considerations put forward in your
note of the 19th applicable to all Sections of the
Secretariat? I think so. As however the
invitation of the Swiss Federal Government has been
accepted it would seem necessary for the Secretariat
to be represented - If it is agreed that a member
of the Health Section would be most suitable Dr Rajchman
desires me to propose myself.
[Signature] Norman White
12/3/29

I certainly have no objections.
[Signature] E.C.
12/3/29

Mr. Walters.
[Signature] E.C.

Seen, many thanks.

The final date seems to be still unfixed, & we
cannot therefore write definitely at present.

Dame R. Crowdy might be interested to see on her return
to Geneva.
[Signature] HWalters
13/3

Seen by Dame R. Crowdy who has no observations
[Signature] JAD 26/4
