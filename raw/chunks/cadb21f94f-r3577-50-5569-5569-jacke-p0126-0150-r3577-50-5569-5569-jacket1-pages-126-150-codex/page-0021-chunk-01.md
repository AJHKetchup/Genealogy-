## Literal Transcription

```
                                                                I
                                                    50/5569/5569.

To the SECRETARY GENERAL.

          Don't you think that the Geneva Red Cross
Society is an "International Bureau"? If I am
right in this surmise, don't you think that the
letter of the Département Politique Fédéral of
the 2nd July 1928 ought to have passed to the
International Bureaux Section "for action?"

          I know it is not always easy to define what
is and what is not an "International Bureau."
But I hope, that gradually it will become general
knowledge that there is such a thing as an
International Bureau Section in the Secretariat.
see file
5A/11022/11022                                      Albert Dufour
for SG's note


20th March 1929.
```
