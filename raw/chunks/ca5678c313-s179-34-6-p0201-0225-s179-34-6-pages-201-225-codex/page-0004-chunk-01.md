## Literal Transcription

#30

(Montevideo, #4)
June 23, 1924

Mr. Walfe, police interpreter, so-called, assigned to assist
us by the Chief of Police, told me confidentially that we should not
expect to get from the Police Department information on all the cases
involving the traffic and kaftans, because some of the police officials
on account of their connection with the traffickers, would not care to
divulge any real information.

He said that to his own knowledge there is a traffic in
women going on; and that he has the names of several of the traffickers
which he would give to me later (however, he failed to do this).
He said that a certain lawyer named Romeis, who stands very high in
official circles here, owns a disorderly house, and is in contact with
traffickers and their associates.

I asked him if he knew anything about the reporter of La
Tribuna who was killed several months ago at the instigation of traffickers,
because this reporter started an expose in the paper about
the methods used. (This was reported by #70). Mr. Wolf informed me
that he did not know about this, but would make inquiries and
let me know later. Later he told me that about three years ago a reporter
of La Tribuna, named Aldalasaco de Dios was shot at by a man
named De Maria who was a secret agent of the Police; that he did this
because the reporter was trying to expose the graft, etc., in the
Police Department in their relations with the prostitutes.
