On June 20th, 1924, Maj. Johnson and I accompanied by Dr.
Jose A. Mora Otero of the League of Nations Division of the Uruguayan
Ministry of Foreign Relations, called on Sr. Juan C. Gomez Folle, the
Chief of Police of Montevideo, but owing to the lateness of the hour
of our visit, and other difficulties, not much could be discussed.

would call on us the same evening and arrange for an appointment for
the following day.

On the evening of the same day, a Mr. Wolf, interpreter

next Saturday he would call and take us to Headquarters for a con-
ference with his Chief.
