In some resorts the madams charge 15 pesos a week for
board and lodging, while in others the inmates do not reside, but
merely come to the house daily to go to work.

was more than eager for another inmate. She introduced me to two
of her four inmates, namely S-G and S-C; she said "You see why I must
have other girls. These two ladies are my sisters. They came all
the way from France to see me. They have been here for six months,
and now they leave me to go home". I said "I suppose they made ex-
penses?"; the madame replied "Of course, they make plenty of money.
