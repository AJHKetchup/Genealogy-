NOTE BY THE
SECRETARY GENERAL
There is evidently a continuance of the misunderstanding,
since
our letter of June 27th is clearly intended to propose
that
the arrangements by which Honduras should continue until
1932 to
pay a half unit should be maintained,
and it is there¬
fore,
so far as we are concerned, completely satisfactory
that the budget should provide for this amount. It would
therefore, I think, be good if M. Nogueira could write
urgently and explain this matter once more.
That
passage of the letter which bears on arrears should
go to the Treasurer through Marquis Paulucci.
I
am glad to
see that the
overseas sheet prepared by
the Information Section is proving useful,

some doubts
were expressed, particularly as to its effectiveness in Latin-
American countries.
I
agree with what M.
Nogueira writes as to suggesting
to

Ribas thet he should deal when required with matters
