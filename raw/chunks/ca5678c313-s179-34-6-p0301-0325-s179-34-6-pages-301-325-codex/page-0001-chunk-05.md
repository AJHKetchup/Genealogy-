there who calls herself "Mina", and he showed me her cedula de identificacion and her Prostitution Booklet which showed the following:

<u>Cedula</u>: #16392; Police Registration #148747; name, Yuana
Maria Torres de Perez; born in Juin, San Jose, Uruguay, March 17, 1904;
color, white; fingerprint #43343, I 2222; cedula issued Jan. 31, 1924.

<u>Prostitution Booklet</u>: Health Dept. Registration #6083; date
of inscription, Feb. 7, 1924; name, Yuana M. Torres de Perez; nation-
ality, Uruguayan; age, 20 years; married; file #2046. Since Feb. 7,
1924, to July 1, 1924, she was examined medically 42 times; and pre-
viously was a prostitute at the following places: Feb. 7, 1924, at
Calle Iaguron; Feb. 9, Calle Soriano 817; May 29, Juncal 1266.
On ^the^ date of registration for prostitution, this woman was
19 years, 10 months and 20 days of age.

I was informed by Mr. Wolf that the Police can take no ac-
tion against this woman, because although it is prohibited for a woman
under 21 years of age to practice prostitution, the law exempts mar-
ried women, and this woman, being married, is within the law. The
Prostitution Booklet and Cedula were returned to her.
