  (CIDA), the Canadian Cooperative Association (CCA), the Deutsche Gesellschaft fur

  others.
- Experience working in multicultural, multilingual and interdisciplinary contexts.
- Excellent interpersonal skills, team player and highly motivated.
- Areas of expertise include: local and rural development including enterprise building;
  agricultural research and extension, communication, technology transfer and rural
  financial services; poverty reduction strategies; development of indigenous groups and
  community participation in rural development; evaluation, institutional assessments,
  social and economic impact assessments; corporate social responsibility and conflict
  resolution in the context of extractive industries; environment and community forestry.

SUMMARY OF EXPERIENCE

2010 to date
Regional Rural Development Training Programme - PROCASUR Corporation
Advisor

  Central Africa, ii) drafting a report on suggested best practices for knowledge management
