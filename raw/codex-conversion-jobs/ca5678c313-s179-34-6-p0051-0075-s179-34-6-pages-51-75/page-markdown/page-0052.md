Section IV. The Corruption of Minors.

Art. 297. Anyone who, in order to gratify another's
lusts, shall urge or incite a woman under twenty-one
to prostitute herself, shall be punished with imprison-
ment for from fifteen to eighteen months.

The penalty shall be increased if the offence is
committed:

1. with a girl under twelve;
2. by means of cunning or deception;
3. by relations in the direct ascending line or
   by fosterfathers parents;
4. by a person who is in charge of the minor
   as guardian, tutor or custodian, even
   if only temporarily.

Art. 298. Anyone who, without inciting to prostitution,
shall encourage or facilitate it by the means and in the
circumstances referred to in the preceding article,
shall be liable to imprisonment for from twelve to
fifteen months.

Section V.

Regulations applying to the foregoing sections.
Art. 299. The crimes of violation and rape abduction shall also be
punished by fines:

1. To be paid as compensation to the victim if
   she is an unmarried person or a widow;
2. To provide alimony maintenance to any issue the woman
   may have, in accordance with the provisions of the
   Civil Code.
