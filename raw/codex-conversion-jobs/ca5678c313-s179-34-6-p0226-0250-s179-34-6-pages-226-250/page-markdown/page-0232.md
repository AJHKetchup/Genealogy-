(Montevideo, #21)

I'll be glad to look them up. Do you know anybody who does su-
shieking (sending of girls)?"; she said "No, we never bother that
way. You got to send a ticket and then you sometimes loose the girl
before she pays her money back. I can always get one in Buenos Aires
at the Casino, or here. They come around looking for a place".

I said "I'll tell you why I came here: A friend of mine
is a friend of 15-G, and he told me that she could tip me off to
something good in France for my girl"; she replied "15-G is a German
girl. I dont think she knows anything about France. I'll call her
in. You can speak to her".

I was then introduced to 15-G; after explaining who had
sent me, she remembered the man distinctly and said "I never worked
in France. My home is in Berlin. My whole family is there. That
is the only place I know". I said to her "Do you like Montevideo?";
she replied "No, there are too many girls here to make much money".
I said "Why did you come then?"; she said "Things are very bad in
Germany. I thought I could make good money here". I then said "Have
you been here long?"; she answered "Seven months". I said "Did you
come here directly?"; she replied "No, I went to Rio and then came
here. I took a German line(steamship). I could not get a visas
for this country". I said "How did you get here, then?"; she said
"By train".

15-G is an admitted pervert and claims to be 24 years of
age. She is 5'6" tall; weighs about 130 lbs; admittedly 24 years of
age; light brown hair, bobbed and parted on left side, and marcelled;
blue eyes; fair complexion; even spaced yellow teeth; wore a blue
fur-trimmed kimona.

While in Madame S-M's house at 817 Canelones Calle, we
discussed the departure of madame's two sisters to France on July 21.
