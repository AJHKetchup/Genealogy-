(Montevideo, #25)

About 9:30 P.M. I visited the wharf and observed the de-
parture of the ^S.S. Cuidad de Montevideo for Buenos Aires. I noticed that
the Chief Purser refused to allow passengers to board the steamer
until he had very thoroughly looked at their passports or cedulas.
Persons not having passports were not permitted to board the boat to
bid friends good bye. And persons who had passports were requested
to show also certificates of good moral character, as well as a gener-
al health certificate.
