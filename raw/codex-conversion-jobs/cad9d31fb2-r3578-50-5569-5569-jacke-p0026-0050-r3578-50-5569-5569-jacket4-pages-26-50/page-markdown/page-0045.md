Art. 13

The Italian Delegation shares the point of view of the Polish
Delegation - viz. in case of very serious illness which do require
special and long treatments or important surgical operations, the
prisoners should be conveyed to the Hospital which belong to the
Military forces or to the Hospital where civilians are treated.

The Italian Delegation also calls the attention of the
Commission to the fact that during the last war belligerants did
exchange prisoners affected by consumption, who were reduced in
miserable conditions as a consequence of the absence of a prompt
diagnosis and of the complete absence of care.

Therefore the Italian Delegation proposes to add the following
phrase after the first comma of article 13:

"Specialized medical organisations must be provided for quick-
"ly detecting the beginning of the consumption desease in every
"camp or depot or group of depot of prisoners of war".

The Italian Delegation is also of the opinion that the
doctors captured must be appointed to the depots where are col-
lected the prisoners who belong to their country in order to assure
their medical assistance.
