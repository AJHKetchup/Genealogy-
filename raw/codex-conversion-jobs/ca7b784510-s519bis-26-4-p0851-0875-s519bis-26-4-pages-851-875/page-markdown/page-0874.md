## p.TTRg-

## M. ROGUEIRA

As you are aware, a special allowance of Fr 50 per day has been conceded to Mile Paradas, Correspondent of the Secretariat in Santa Domingo, for days spent on duty on land while in Europe.

The maximum period, however, which Hie Paradas was supposed to spend in Europe does not appear to he fixed. It would consequently he necessary to settle the question of the special allowance for Mlle Paradas, as it would he advisable to pass all the accounts in hand concerning Mlle Paradas at the same time. Other¬ wise she would appear to he a debtor to the Secretariat, whereas she is entitled to draw this special allowance. Could you kindly give me your view as to the period during which the indeipnity in question of Pr 50 per day is to he paid now to Mlle Paradas. I beg to attach the proper form by which the payment should he made and M. Rodriguez in his quality of Chief of the Latin American Liaison would perhaps kindly approve it. It should he noted that Mlle Paradas is not entitled to her honorarium which is fixed for her stay in South America, until her return, as her stay in Europe will he covered by the special allowance.
