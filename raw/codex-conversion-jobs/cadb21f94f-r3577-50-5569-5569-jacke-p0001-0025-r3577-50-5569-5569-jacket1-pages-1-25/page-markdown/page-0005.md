## Literal Transcription
50/5569/5569

M. DUFOUR
m

This Conference which meets in Geneva is one of
which the general importance and interest is so
great that I should be glad if you would formally
represent me at it.

Perhaps at the same time we might suggest
that we should be grateful if facilities for
following the work of the Conference and its
Commissions could be given to two other members
of the Secretariat, Dr. Norman White and M.
Pietromarchi, since the latter has attended a
number of Red Cross meetings on behalf of the
Secretariat.

d'accord
R.B.C.

27th April 1929.
RD

Marquis Paulucci )
Dr. Norman White ) to see tho

P.O.R.C.S.
