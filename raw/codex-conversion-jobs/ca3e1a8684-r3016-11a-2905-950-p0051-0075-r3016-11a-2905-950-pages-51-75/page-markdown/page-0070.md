## Literal Transcription
```
                                                                11A/2905/950. 10a
                                                                October 13th, 1928.

Monsieur PIETROMARCHI.

I fear I could not agree to the expenses of Dr.
Kulz at the Red Cross meeting at the Hague being
defrayed out of League funds.

As you will doubtless remember, it was only with
considerable difficulty that the Supervisory Commission
accepted the Secretary-General's proposal that the
sum of 15,000 francs should be included in the 1928
budget for the Ciraolo scheme. The Commission finally
agreed on the understanding that "an estimate of
15,000 francs has been included in the Budget
for a meeting in 1928 of the Preparatory Committee
of Enquiry into the Scheme for an International
Federation for Mutual Assistance in the Relief
of Peoples overtaken by Disaster."

                                                                J.C. Buile
```
