## Literal Transcription
Paredes

M. Nogueira.

Thank you for your note of the 16th with
reference to the letter from M. Paredes.

The reply to the question put in the second
part of the minute is to be found in clause III of
Proposal V adopted by the Co-ordination Committee
on October 19th. It empowers the Committee of
XVIII to afford if necessary the assistance
contemplated at the beginning of paragraph 2 of
that Proposal.

It would be well to add, however, that the
Committee of XVIII has not been seized by any
demands and that most Governments have thought
it wiser to endeavour to obtain such support as
they may need by bilateral negotiations. I may
add that I am most anxious that the Committee of
XVIII should not be appealed to in this connection
as it would only lead to a scrummage of the most
undesirable description.

Alonay

17th December, 1935.

Not for Registry.
