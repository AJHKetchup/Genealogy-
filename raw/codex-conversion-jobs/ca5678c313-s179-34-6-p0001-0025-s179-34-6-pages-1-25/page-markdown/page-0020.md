## Literal Transcription

(Uruguay, S.A.)
EXHIBIT F.
Montevideo, July 3rd, 1924

Dr. Arturo Arechevaleto,
Interventor de Colonias
Casa de Imigracion, Montevideo.

Dear Sir:-

On leaving Uruguay, as I do on July 4th, I wish to express my appre-
ciation of the real cooperation and courtesies which I have received at your
hands and also from all of your staff with whom I have come into contact,
particularly Mr. Enrique Cohe, Chief Inspector of Disembarkment.

Thanking you for your past and future cooperation, I remain

Very truly yours,

(Signed) BASCOM JOHNSON
Representing the Special Commission
to Investigate the International
Traffic in Women and Children in
Collaboration with Governments.
