## Literal Transcription

19
(Uruguay, S.A.)
EXHIBIT G.

Montevideo, July 3rd, 1924

Dr. Alfredo Vidal y Fuentes
Presidente del Consejo Nacional de Higiene
Montevideo, Uruguay

My dear Dr. Vidal y Fuentes:-

As I am leaving Uruguay on July 4th, I wish to express my deep appre-
ciation of the real cooperation and unfailing courtesy which I have received
at your hands and also from all of your staff with whom I have come into contact
particularly Dr. Julio Etchepare, Inspector de Sanidad Terrestre and Dr. Abel
I. Zamora of the Dispensario de la Prostitución.

Will you be kind enough to remind Dr. Zamora of his promise to my
assistant, Mr. Auerbach, to have prepared information for the past ten (10)
years showing the number of registered houses, the total number of registered
active prostitutes for each year and the number of the new inscriptions by
nationalities for each year. I have already received the number of new
inscriptions by nationalities since the year 1919 through the kindness of Dr.
Etchepare. Dr. Zamora has also sent me a chart showing the number of those
examined both within and outside the dispensary and those hospitalized in
each class.

I should greatly appreciate receiving the remainder of the statistics
above mentioned at my hotel, Hotel Copacabana, at Rio de Janeiro on or before
July 23rd when I leave for New York.

Very respectfully yours,

(Signed)
BASCOM JOHNSON
Representing the Special Commission
to Investigate the International
Traffic in Women and Children in
Collaboration with Governments.
