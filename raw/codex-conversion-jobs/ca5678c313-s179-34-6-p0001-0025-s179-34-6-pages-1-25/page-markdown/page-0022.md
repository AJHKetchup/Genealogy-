## Literal Transcription

```
                                                                                                20
(Uruguay, S.A.,                                          EXHIBIT H.

                                                                        Montevideo, July 3d, 1924

Chief of Police,
Montevideo, Uruguay.

Sir:

          On leaving Uruguay, as I do July 4th, I wish to express
my deep appreciation of the real cooperation and unfailing courtesy
which I have received at your hands and also from all of your staff
with whom I have come into contact. I desire particularly to empha-
size the intelligence and good will shown by Mr. Carlos Ferrando,
secretary-general of the Police Department, who has shown me every
facility and promised full compliance with my requests for information.

          I should also like to bear witness to the faithful and ac-
tive services of Mr. Rodolfo S. Wolf whom you have assigned to assist
me.

          The last questionnaire dated July 1st has been given to the
Minister of Foreign Affairs for official transmission to you, an ad-
vance copy having been left with Mr. Carlos Ferrando who has promised
to send me either here in Montevideo before July 4th or at my hotel
Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro, complete replies to the same.

          Thanking you again for past courtesies and future co-
operation, I remain

                                                                Yours respectfully,


                                                                (Signed) BASCOM JOHNSON
                                                                Representing the Special Commission
                                                                to Investigate the International
                                                                Traffic in Women and Children in
                                                                Collaboration with Governments.
```
