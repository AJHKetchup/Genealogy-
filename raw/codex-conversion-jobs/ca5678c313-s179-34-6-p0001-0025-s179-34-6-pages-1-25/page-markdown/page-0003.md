```
- 1 - July 7, 1926

URUGUAY, S. A.

I. _Time of Investigation:_ June 19 - July 4, 1924.
II. _Places Visited:_ Montevideo and Salto.
III. _Persons Interviewed; and Methods Used:_ On June 19, 1924,
the investigator presented his credentials at the office of the Minister
of Foreign Affairs, Sr. Manini. Pending an interview with the Minister,
Dr. Jose Mora O Tero, attached to the League of Nations Division, made
arrangements for an interview with the Chief of Police of the Monte-
video District, Sr. Juan C. Gomez Folle. Sr. Folle was appointed by
the Minister of the Interior, as were the Chiefs of all the other Police
Districts throughout Uruguay. The Montevideo Police District included
not only the city proper, but an area outside about ten times as large.
This area included a large majority of the country's population and its
only important port of entry. Its Chief was considered by the Minister
as his expert adviser on police matters throughout the country.

At the interview with Chief Folle, to which the investigator
was conducted by Sr. Otero, Chief Folle requested that a list of ques-
tions be prepared and submitted. Two such lists were prepared. The first
of these was transmitted direct and answered in writing by Sr. Carlos
Ferrando, the Secretary-General of the Police Department. The second was
transmitted through the Minister of Foreign Affairs, but discussed in-
formally with Sr. Ferrando, with whom a copy was left, pending its formal

receipt. A written reply to this second list has not yet been received.
The two sets of questions and the reply to the first are attached here-
to and marked _Exhibits A, B and C_, respectively.
```
