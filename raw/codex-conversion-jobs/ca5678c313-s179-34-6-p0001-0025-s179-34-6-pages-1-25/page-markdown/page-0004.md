        The next day Sr. Otero presented the investigator to Dr.
Alfredo Vidal y Fuentes, President of the National Council of Hygiene,
and to his Chief Inspector, Dr. Julio Etchepare. The Council of Hy-
giene has control over all sanitary matters connected with prostitu-
tion throughout the country. At this and subsequent conferences with
these and other officials of the Council considerable information
both oral and documentary was furnished, including statistics of in-
scriptions of prostitutes by nationality, attached hereto and marked
<u>Exhibit D</u>.

        Visits were also made to the Dispensary and Hospital where
prostitutes were examined and treated, and conferences held with
their respective Chiefs.

        Similar calls were made on the Immigration Department and
several conferences held with its officials, including Dr. Arturo
Arechevalete, Interventor de Colonias, and Chief Inspector of Dis-
embarkation, Sr. Enrique Cohe.

        An investigator was also sent to Salto near the Argentine
border on the Uruguayan river. He spent four days there investigat-
ing reports that wholesale clandestine entry into Argentine was ac-
complished at that point.

        Visits were also made to 147 houses of prostitution in
Montevideo and many of the inmates and manageresses were interviewed.
In addition many souteneurs and men about town were consulted about
traffic conditions. Letters were transmitted to the chief officials
visited through the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, thanking them for
their assistance and, in some cases, requesting further information.
Copies of these letters are attached hereto and marked <u>Exhibits E, F, G</u>,
and <u>H</u>, respectively.
