Note for Mr.Renborg.

– Please find enclosed an extract from the Bulletin
of the Department of State for July 22nd containing the
exchange of notes between the Governments of the United
States and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
concerning the limitation of the production of opium.

– 2. I am enclosing also an extract from the Revista
Medica of Colombia containing an article entitled "El
Cocaismo en Colombia" and a copy of the 1945 "Disposi-
ciones sobre Comercio y Control de Estupefacientes."

– 3. Annual Reports for 1944 from French West Africa
and Reunion are attached.

– 4. There are also attached two seizure reports
from Mexico and a report from the Portuguese Government
concerning seizures in Portuguese India in December 1944.

Washington, D.C.,
August 23rd, 1945.

(signed) L. Steinig.

Bertil A. Renborg, Esquire,
Chief, Drug Control Service,
League of Nations,
Geneva, Switzerland.

LEAGUE OF NATIONS
REGISTRY
RECEIVED
