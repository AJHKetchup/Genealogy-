A PROCLAMATION

WHEREAS, a Convention Relating to the Treatment of Prisoners
of War was signed by the respective Plenipotentiaries of the United
States of America and forty-six other countries, at Geneva on
July 27, 1929, the original of which Convention in the French
language is word for word as follows:

DE GUERRE DU 27 JUILLET 1929.
