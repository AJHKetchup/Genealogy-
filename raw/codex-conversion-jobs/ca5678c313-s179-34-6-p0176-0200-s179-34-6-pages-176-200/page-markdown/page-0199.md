she works (practices prostitution). No photograph nor fingerprint is
kept in the Health Department, but if the prostitute is registered,
she must present herself or submit herself twice a week to a physician
of the Health Department for medical examination.

After registering with the Health Department and having re-
ceived a booklet, she then goes to the Sectional or District Police
Station and informs them as to where she expects to work. The same
facts as in the Health Department are asked for and entered in a book
at the Police Station. There is a cross-index by name and address
kept here also.
