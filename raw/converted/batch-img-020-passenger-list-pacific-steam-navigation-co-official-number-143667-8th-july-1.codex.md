## Interpretation

This passenger list records Dario Pulgar A. and Dorothy Pulgar as alien passengers arriving at Plymouth from Talcahuano, Chile, on the visible date of July 6, 1928. Dario is listed as a medical surgeon, age 39, with a proposed London address care of Anglo South American Bank Ltd. Dorothy appears immediately below him, age 28, with ditto marks tying her address and country columns to the preceding row. The manifest title says July 8, 1928, while the visible page header reads July 6, 1928; preserve both until the archive citation can be checked.
